These 3D rendered images enabled the game development team to better understand the mood, setting, and environment of the game.

{% for image in page.image_gallery_2 %} {% endfor %}

Helion, a space arcade shooter made for the iOS platform, was a throwback to the simplistic addictive fun of arcade games. A modern-day visual treat, with lush artwork, BRDF shading, and particle effects pushed the device hardware to the limit.

  • Created assets- 3D models, high-poly sculpts, environments, sprites, and textures for Helion and created concept artwork for Darkrail and SuperWTF
  • Managed and guided a team of two artists
  • Worked alongside graphics programmers giving artistic feedback on shaders, particle systems, sprites, and the game editor design interface.
