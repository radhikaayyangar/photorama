Non-commissioned 3d sculpts undertaken as a study in anatomical modelling. Created in Zbrush.
Inspired by Lovecraftian lore, I wanted to create a juvenile version of Cthulhu, the “The Great Old One”.
Here I attempted to capture an instance from his childhood, perhaps momentarily bored after frolicking through the depths of the ocean, terrorizing beings and leaving chaos in his wake.
